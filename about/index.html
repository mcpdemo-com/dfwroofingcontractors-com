Who We Are

A DFW Contractor Network Built for Homeowners

DFW Roofing Contractors is a Dallas-Fort Worth based referral network connecting homeowners with pre-screened, licensed, and insured local roofing contractors. We exist because finding a trustworthy roofer in the DFW metro shouldn't be a gamble.

Storm chaser crews roll into DFW after every major hail event. They're here today, gone tomorrow — leaving homeowners with voided manufacturer warranties, improper installations, and no recourse when problems appear six months later. We only work with contractors who are established in DFW, carry full insurance, and stand behind their work.

When you submit a request through our site, it goes directly to our contractor network — no auction to the highest bidder, no reselling your information to out-of-state call centers. A real, local DFW contractor contacts you.
