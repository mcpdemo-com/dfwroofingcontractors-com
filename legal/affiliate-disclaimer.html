Affiliate & Lead Generation Disclaimer

Last updated: April 10, 2026

Plain-language summary: When you fill out a form on this site, we share your information with licensed DFW roofing contractors. Those contractors may pay us a fee for the referral. We only work with licensed, insured contractors and we never sell your information to unrelated third parties.

What We Are

DFW Roofing Contractors (dfwroofingcontractors.com) operates as a lead generation and contractor referral service. We are not a licensed roofing contractor and we do not perform roofing services. Our business model connects homeowners who need roofing services with licensed, insured roofing contractors operating in the Dallas-Fort Worth metro area.

How We Generate Revenue

We may receive compensation from roofing contractors in our network when we refer a homeowner to them. This compensation may take the form of:

  • A per-lead fee paid by a contractor upon receiving your contact information
  • A monthly retainer paid by a contractor for exclusive or preferred placement
  • A commission or referral fee based on completed jobs

This compensation does not affect the price you pay for any roofing services. Contractors set their own pricing independently.

How Your Information Is Used

When you submit a form on this Site, the information you provide — including your name, phone number, email address, property address, and service description — is shared with one or more contractors in our network for the purpose of providing you with a roofing estimate.

By submitting a form, you expressly consent to being contacted by these contractors by phone, email, or text message. Standard message and data rates may apply for text communications.

What We Do Not Do

  • We do not sell your information to data brokers or lead aggregators outside our network
  • We do not share your information with out-of-state companies or storm-chaser crews
  • We do not share your information with more contractors than necessary to fulfill your request
  • We do not use your information for unrelated marketing purposes

Contractor Independence

All contractors in our network are independent businesses. DFW Roofing Contractors does not control their pricing, scheduling, work quality, or customer service. While we vet contractors for licensing and insurance, we do not guarantee the outcome of any work performed.

FTC Compliance

This disclaimer is provided in accordance with the Federal Trade Commission's guidelines on endorsements and testimonials, and in compliance with applicable state and federal consumer protection laws. We are committed to transparency about our business model.

Questions

If you have questions about this disclaimer or how your information is handled, contact us at info@dfwroofingcontractors.com or visit our Contact page.
